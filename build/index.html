Welcome to the leeds hunters

The Leeds Hunters RUFC is an inclusive rugby club. The Club's primary aim is to provide, for any adult male aged 18 or over irrespective of race, sexual orientation, ethnicity and level of fitness or experience, a safe environment in which to enjoy the game of rugby. For all members, players, supporters and volunteers, The Leeds Hunters will always strive to foster an environment of mutual respect, equality, tolerance and teamwork, as well as promote cooperation, friendship and compassion both on and off the pitch.

The Game

In Summary rugby is a game in which the objective is to carry the ball over the opponents’ try line and force it to the ground to score.

However in order to move forwards the rugby ball must be passed backwards, this means the team needs to work as one unit in order to move the ball forwards towards the opponents’ try line, in order to score and go on to win the match. As well as passing the ball backwards it is possible to kick the ball forwards however, the entire kicker team must behind the ball at the moment it is kicked.

A team is broken up in to forwards (1-8) and backs (9-15), who work together with the different skill sets to reach the try line. Forwards are usually the bigger / stronger guys on the pitch, involved with the scrums and line outs, whereas the backs are usually the fastest on the pitch.

Scoring Points

Try - 5 Points

A try is scored when the ball is grounded over the oppositions' try line in the goal area (before the dead zone). A Penalty try may be given if the opposition team have stopped the player getting a try by foul play.

Conversion - 2 Points

After a try the team can add another two points by attempting to kick the ball over the goal, the starting positom is based on the where the try was scored.

Penalty - 3 Points

When given a penalty caused by an opposition's team infringement during open play, the team may choose to have goal kick to score another 3 points.

Drop goal - 3 Points

During open play a player can score a drop goal by dropping the ball to the ground and kicking it as it bounces back up over the goal.
